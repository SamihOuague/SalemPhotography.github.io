My Skills

As a student photographer, I've honed a diverse set of skills to bring my vision to life behind the lens.
Proficient in both digital and film photography, I possess a deep understanding of camera settings,
exposure, and composition techniques. My post-processing abilities are refined, allowing me to enhance and refine images to their fullest potential using software such as Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom.
Versatility is key in my practice, as I'm adept at capturing a wide range of subjects, from portraits to landscapes,
events to still life. Additionally, my strong communication skills enable me to connect with clients and collaborators, ensuring that their vision is translated seamlessly into the final product.
